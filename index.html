About

I'm Joshua Knott, a Politics and International Relations undergraduate at the University of Surrey who builds software. My work sits at the intersection of technology, politics, and AI.

Outside of my university course and building software, I lead student societies at Surrey. I am currently the President of the Artificial Intelligence & Data Science Society and Vice President of the Neurotech Society, having previously served as President of the Business Society.

Projects

Memvella

Memvella is a voice-first digital wellness companion built to help families stay connected with seniors. It coordinates organiser/member workspaces with a dedicated tablet experience and independent senior flow, built as a monorepo utilizing Next.js, Convex, and Better Auth.

FounderOS

FounderOS is a private AI-native operating system for solo founders. It provides a universal command surface to ask questions, manage recurring schedules, coordinate background AI workers, and query business knowledge, built with Next.js, Tailwind CSS, Convex, and Clerk.

SurreySocieties

SurreySocieties is a monorepo managing the public website portals and committee admin dashboards for three University of Surrey student organisations: the Artificial Intelligence Society, the Business Society, and the Neurotech Society, built with Astro, Convex, Clerk, and Tailwind CSS.

Polis

Polis is an academic intelligence workspace designed for social science students to organise module materials, analyze complex readings, and build evidence-based arguments with citation-preserving, source-grounded AI, built using Next.js, Convex, and Clerk.
